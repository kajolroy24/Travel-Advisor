About Spain

Spain is a country of stone castles, snowcapped mountains, vast monuments, and sophisticated cities. It is the fourth-largest country in Europe and the second-most visited country in the world. Some of the popular tourist destinations include Madrid, Barcelona, Seville, Valencia, and Granada.
